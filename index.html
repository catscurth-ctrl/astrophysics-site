From Wonder To Theory

This is my journey through mathematics, astrophysics, and physics.

I wondered about aliens when I was younger.

I told my mom about them and she thought about it.

At the time I didn’t care much about space but still loved reading physics books.

I once thought the moon landing was fake… but I realized that wasn’t smart.

Then I discovered my passion for calculus and math became my focus.

I grew fascinated by black holes — their darkness and mystery pulled me in.

My theory suggests black holes and white holes act like a cosmic bridge.

Black holes compress information into 2D, while white holes reconstruct it.

This could explain how information is preserved across the universe.

Cosmic Conclusion

In conclusion, this theory reimagines how information moves through the universe.
